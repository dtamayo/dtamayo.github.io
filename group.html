Group

As research opportunities arise, we recruit research students exclusively through the Harvey Mudd Undergraduate Research Portal. URO rounds typically run in August/September, November/December, and January/February. We encourage applications from students across all the Claremont Colleges.

If you would like to find out more about our work outside of a URO cycle, check out the materials on the Research page, or talk to any of our team below. I'm happy to answer questions over email, but the best time to talk planets is to stop by any of my office hours!
